Help Page

'w', 'up arrow' - up
'a', 'left arrow' - left
's', 'down arrow' - down
'd', 'right arrow' - right

After the beggining of the game on the right screen you will see colorfull bubbles with numbers
and an equation you need to solve in order to load your ship with fuel. Clicking on the bubble with
the number you think is the answer will add to your fuel. Clicking on a wrong answer will remove some
of the fuel.
