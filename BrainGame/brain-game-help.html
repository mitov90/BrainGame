'w', 'up arrow' - move up 'a', 'left arrow' - move left 's', 'down arrow' - move down 'd', 'right arrow' - move right 'mouse', 'left button' - picks equotation After the beggining of the game on the right screen you will see colorfull bubbles with numbers and an equation you need to solve in order to load your ship with fuel. Clicking on the bubble with the number you think is the answer will add to your fuel. Clicking on a wrong answer will remove some of the fuel.
